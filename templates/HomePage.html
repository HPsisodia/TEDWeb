COMING SOON

Coalesce of Ideas

TEDxNITRaipur is a full day conference scheduled in the first week of march 2020, with around 6 live speakers ;curated from the thoughts leaders across the country and aboard also, as well as grassroots speakers sharing their inspiring stories of their personal achievement . It is an initiative by the students of NIT Raipur to bring up a great set of visionaries on a single stage. We aim to bring innovative minds of society and provide inspiration to the people of Raipur and beyond.
