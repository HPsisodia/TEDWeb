COMING SOON

Coalesce of Ideas

TED is a non-profit global community spreading ideas. It welcomes people from every discipline around the world to deliver short but impactful talks, usually of 18 minutes. TED established in 1984 as a conference where topics related to Technology, Entertainment and Design were to be discussed. But today, it covers almost every topic in this planet- science, business, social issues, personality development, and everything that comes to your mind. The talks by the most inspired thinkers, for the community of curious souls is what this platform does. Believing in the power of idea to change attitudes, lives and ultimately the World.

TEDx is a program of independently organized events that brings people together, created in the spirit of TED’s overall mission to discover “ideas worth spreading”. While TED takes a more global approach, TEDx mainly focuses on a local community that concentrates on local voices. TEDx events include live speakers and recorded TED Talks, thereby celebrating locally-driven ideas and elevating them to a global stage. Officialy, the “x” in TEDx stands for independently organized TED events and, its general rules and regulations are provided by the TED Conference.

Propelled by scintillating innovations, and driven by enlightening the youth of advancements, all around, TEDxNITRaipur strives to foster the core idea, “Ideas worth spreading”, among local communities, and bring to light, the local stars, who have been involved in the construction for a better life, for the generations to come. Held every year, since 2017, and amassing rich support, beating previous records, and inspiring people to explore beyond the conventions, TEDxNITRaipur, with its passionate team of stalwarts, endeavor to begin a new edition with a completely novel, invigorating ambience, and avant-garde themes.
